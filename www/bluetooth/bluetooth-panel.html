About

Web Serial API: Modern browser-based serial communication. Requires Chrome/Electron 117+ and paired Bluetooth devices.

Node.js serialport: Traditional serial port access via native modules. Requires device to be bound to /dev/rfcomm0 or similar.

Setup Instructions

  1. Pair your Bluetooth device in OS settings (Linux: bluetoothctl)
  2. For Web Serial API: Click "Select New Device" in Devices tab
  3. For Node.js serialport: Bind device to /dev/rfcomm0
